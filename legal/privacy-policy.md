# Privacy Policy — Techo Buddy

**Last updated:** April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Techo Buddy ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you call our business phone line, submit information through our website, or otherwise interact with our services.

By calling our phone line or submitting your information, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you call our business or schedule a service, we may collect:

- **Contact information**: your name, phone number, and email address (if provided).
- **Property information**: the address of the property you are inquiring about, the type of work needed (repair or replacement), and other roofing details (estimated square footage, material preferences, urgency).
- **Communication content**: voice recordings of your call, transcripts of our conversation, and SMS message history.
- **Technical data**: caller ID, call duration, and call timestamps.

We do not collect Social Security numbers, financial account information, or government identification numbers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Respond to your inquiry and provide a tentative quote for roofing services.
- Schedule a free on-site inspection.
- Send you SMS messages for appointment confirmations, appointment reminders, and follow-up communications related to your inquiry.
- Improve our AI voice assistant and service quality through reviewing call transcripts and recordings.
- Comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information with:

- **The roofing company you contacted** (if Techo Buddy is operating as the AI receptionist on their behalf), so they can perform the inspection and follow up with you.
- **Service providers** that help us operate our service, including:
  - Vapi (voice AI platform)
  - Twilio (telephony and SMS)
  - Deepgram (speech-to-text)
  - OpenAI (large language model)
  - ElevenLabs (text-to-speech)
  - Google (Sheets, Calendar)
  - Notion (lead management database)
  - n8n (workflow automation)

These providers process your information solely to provide services to us and are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations.

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. SMS / Text Messaging

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from us related to your inquiry, including appointment confirmations and reminders. Message frequency varies (typically 1-3 messages per appointment). Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out at any time by replying **STOP** to any of our messages. Reply **HELP** for assistance. Opting out of SMS will not prevent us from following up with you by phone or email regarding your inquiry.

We will not share your mobile information with third parties for marketing purposes. Mobile information will not be shared with affiliates or third parties for promotional purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the services you requested and to comply with our legal obligations, typically up to 2 years. Voice recordings are retained for up to 90 days for quality assurance purposes unless required for a longer period due to a dispute or legal request.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
- Opt out of SMS communications (reply STOP to any message).
- Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information in Section 9.

## 7. Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information, including encryption in transit, access controls on our systems, and confidentiality agreements with our service providers. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
