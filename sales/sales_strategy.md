# Sales Strategy: Never Miss A Call

## 1. Market Analysis & Opportunity
The target markets (Los Angeles, Houston, Miami, San Antonio, Phoenix, Dallas) share a critical characteristic: a massive Spanish-speaking population that is often underserved by traditional English-centric small businesses.

### The Problem
*   **High Lead Cost:** Roofers spend $50-$200 per lead via PPC, Angie’s List, or Thumbtack.
*   **The Language Barrier:** 30-50% of the population in these metros speaks Spanish at home. Many prefer to discuss high-ticket home improvements in their native language.
*   **After-Hours Loss:** 40% of home service inquiries happen outside of 9-5 business hours.
*   **Human Limitations:** Hiring a 24/7 bilingual receptionist costs $5k+/month. Voicemail is where leads go to die.

### Competition
While general AI answering services exist, few are:
1.  **Bilingual by design:** Seamlessly switching to Spanish mid-call.
2.  **Industry-Specific:** Calculating tentative roof quotes on the fly using industry-standard formulas (Living Area * 1.5 multiplier) and current market pricing for Asphalt and Metal/Tile.
3.  **Action-Oriented:** Focused on booking the inspection, not just taking a message. It qualifies leads on urgency, material type, and insurance involvement.
4.  **No-Software Delivery:** Leads are delivered via SMS and Google Sheets, fitting perfectly into the roofer's existing field-based workflow without requiring them to learn new tools.

## 4. Outreach Plan

### Phase 1: Cold Email Sequence (The "Lead Leak" Audit)
*   **Subject:** I called your office after hours (and someone else answered)
*   **Body:** Focus on the fact that I (as a "test caller") couldn't get a quote or speak to someone in Spanish.
*   **Call to Action:** "Can I show you the transcript of what happens when a $15k Spanish lead calls you at 6 PM?"

### Phase 2: Cold Calling / Voice Drop
*   Targeting owners directly.
*   "We're helping roofers in [City] capture the 30% of the market that's currently hanging up because of the language barrier."

### Phase 3: Strategic Partnerships
*   Reaching out to local SEO agencies that work with roofers. They are frustrated because their leads aren't being closed. We are the "missing link" that makes their marketing look better.
