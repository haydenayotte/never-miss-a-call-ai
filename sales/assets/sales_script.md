# Sales Script: The "Never Miss A Call" Discovery & Closing Call

## Phase 1: The Opener (The "Lead Leak" Hook)
**Sales:** "Hey [Owner Name], this is [Your Name]. I was looking at your site and noticed you're doing some great work in [City]. I actually tried calling your office at 6:30 PM yesterday just to see how your intake process works for Spanish-speaking leads. I got a standard English voicemail."

**Owner:** "Yeah, we're closed then / My girl only speaks English."

**Sales:** "Exactly. And here’s the thing—in [City], about 40% of the homeowners are Spanish-preferred. When they call at 6:30 PM and hear an English voicemail, they don't leave a message. They hang up and call the guy below you on Google who *does* answer. You’re basically paying for your competitors' leads. Does that bother you as much as it bothers me?"

---

## Phase 2: Discovery (The Math of Loss)
**Sales:** "Quick question—what's your average ticket for a full roof replacement right now? $15k? $20k?"

**Owner:** "Around $18,000."

**Sales:** "And what's your profit margin on that after materials and labor? Usually around 25-30%?"

**Owner:** "Yeah, about $5k profit."

**Sales:** "Okay. So if you miss just **one** Spanish-speaking lead a month—just one—that’s $5,000 in pure profit walking out the door. If you miss two, that’s $10,000. Over a year, that’s a brand new truck you just gave to your competitor. How many calls do you think you’re actually missing after hours or from Spanish speakers?"

---

## Phase 3: The Solution (The "Bilingual Closer")
**Sales:** "What we’ve built is a 'Never Miss A Call' system specifically for roofers in [City]. 

Here is how it works:
1. **24/7 Answering:** It answers every call on the first ring, even at 3 AM.
2. **Instant Bilingual Switch:** If they speak Spanish, the AI instantly switches to a native Spanish voice. No 'press 2 for Spanish'—it just happens.
3. **The 'Instant Quote' Hook:** It doesn't just take a message. It asks for their address and home square footage, then uses our proprietary formula to estimate the roof size and provide a tentative quote range (e.g., $12k - $14k for a standard asphalt roof) *live* during the call.
4. **The Booking:** Once they have the quote and they're excited, the AI looks at your calendar and books the on-site inspection right then and there.
5. **No-Software Delivery:** You don't need to learn any new software. The moment the call ends, you get a text message with all the details—name, address, quote, and appointment time. Everything is also synced to a simple Google Sheet for your records. It fits right into your existing workflow."

---

## Phase 4: Closing (The No-Brainer ROI)
**Sales:** "We aren't a 'software' company. We are a lead-recovery service. 

If our system helps you close just **one** extra job every three months, you’ve already tripled your investment. Everything else—the other 10 or 20 leads we capture for you—is pure, found money. 

I’m not asking you to change your marketing. Keep doing what you’re doing. I just want to put a bucket under the leak in your current system. Can we set up a 10-minute demo to show you what the AI sounds like in Spanish?"
