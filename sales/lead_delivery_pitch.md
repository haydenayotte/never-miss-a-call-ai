# The "No-Software" Lead Delivery Pitch

## Core Concept
Roofing owners are busy. They are often on-site, on ladders, or driving between jobs. They don't want to log into a complex CRM or learn new software. 

We deliver leads where they already live: **SMS (Text) and Google Sheets.**

---

## 1. The "Text-to-Lead" Hook
"When our AI finishes a call, you don't need to log in anywhere. You get a text message instantly with the lead's name, address, their tentative quote, and the appointment time they booked. It’s like having a receptionist who texts you every time she hangs up the phone."

### Sample SMS Alert:
> 🏠 **New AI Lead:** Jose Martinez
> 📍 **Address:** 123 Maple St, Houston
> 💰 **Est. Quote:** $18,500 - $21,000 (Asphalt)
> 📅 **Inspection:** Tomorrow @ 10:00 AM
> 📝 **Notes:** Insurance claim pending. Roof is 15 years old.
> 🔗 **Recording:** [Link]

---

## 2. The "Simple Spreadsheet" Management
"All your leads are automatically synced to a private Google Sheet. No passwords, no dashboards, no learning curve. Just a simple list you can share with your team or use to track your sales. If you can use a calculator, you can manage these leads."

---

## 3. Why This Wins (Over Competitors)
1.  **Zero Learning Curve:** No onboarding calls for software training.
2.  **Instant Action:** Leads hit their phone while they are "hot," making it easier to confirm the appointment.
3.  **Low Friction:** Owners don't feel "owned" by a software platform; they just feel supported by a service.

---

## 4. ROI Re-focused (The "Found Money" Proof)
"Because there's no software setup fee and no complex CRM to maintain, your ROI starts the moment the first call is answered. You aren't paying for 'tools'—you're paying for **booked inspections.**"

---

## 5. Handling the "I'm not tech-savvy" Objection
"Good. You don't need to be. If you can read a text message, you can grow your business with this. We handle 100% of the tech; you just handle the inspections."
